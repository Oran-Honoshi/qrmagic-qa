Frequently Asked Questions

Everything people ask before switching to QR Magic.

Is QR Magic really free — no hidden costs?
Yes. The free plan is free forever with no watermark, no trial period, and no credit card required. You can create up to 50 static QR codes and 1 dynamic QR code. Upgrade to Basic ($4/mo) or Plus ($14/mo) when you need more.
What's the difference between static and dynamic QR codes?
A static QR code permanently encodes a URL or data — you can't change it after printing. A dynamic QR code uses a redirect, so you can change the destination URL anytime from your dashboard — without reprinting. Dynamic codes also give you scan analytics (how many people scanned, when, and on which device).
Do my QR codes expire?
Static QR codes never expire — the data is permanently baked in. Dynamic QR codes remain active as long as your QR Magic account is active. On the free plan, dynamic codes stay active indefinitely.
Can I add my logo to a QR code?
Yes — on all plans including free. Upload any PNG, JPG, or SVG logo and it overlays in the center of your QR code. We recommend setting Error Correction Level to "H" (High) when adding a logo, which ensures the QR is still scannable even with a large logo covering the center.
What QR code types does QR Magic support?
QR Magic supports 18 QR code types: URL, Wi-Fi, vCard (contact card), Text, Email, SMS, Phone, WhatsApp, Location / Google Maps, Event (iCal), Social media profiles, YouTube, App Store / Google Play, Bitcoin & crypto, Zoom meeting, PDF / File link, PayPal, and Image URL.
Is QR Magic suitable for my restaurant / real estate business / event?
Absolutely. QR Magic is used by restaurants for digital menus, real estate agents for property listings, event organizers for invitations and schedules, and small businesses for business cards and product packaging. The dynamic QR feature means you update the link without reprinting — saving time and money.
How do I know which of my QR codes is performing best?
Every dynamic QR code shows scan count, click count, and CTR (click-through rate) in your dashboard. The Analytics page shows a weekly breakdown, device types (iOS, Android, Desktop), and a ranking of your top-performing codes. Advanced analytics (heatmap, time analysis, return visitors) are available on paid plans.
Can I use QR Magic on my phone as an app?
Yes. QR Magic is a Progressive Web App (PWA). On Android, Chrome will prompt you to "Add to Home Screen." On iOS Safari, tap Share → "Add to Home Screen." The app opens full-screen like a native app — no App Store download required.
